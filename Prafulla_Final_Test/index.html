Captain America History

Creation

Created in 1940 by Joe Simon.

Debuted in 1941 - Captain America Comics #1.

Team Cap

Over the years, Captain America has had multiple team members.

His first team was called the Howling Commandos and had his best friend Bucky Barnes.
